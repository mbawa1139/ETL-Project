PROJECT REPORT

The initial datasets were taken from Kaggle Open Datasets (link) detailing UK Car Accidents from 2005 to 2015. The three datasets used contained the following information; accident incidents, accient casualities, and vehicles involved. These datasets are formatted as CSV files and can be downloaded here (link).

The inital Accident Incident CSV was first filtered to include only accidents occuring from 2010 forward. The index from this was then matched to both the Casuality Incident CSV and Vehicle Incident CSV, in order to merge both Gender, Age and Vehicle statistics into a new dataset.

The transformed dataset was then again filtered to only include the relevant datapoints to our inital query. The final dataset only includes Motorcycle accidents that show the location, gender and age of victim involved.
